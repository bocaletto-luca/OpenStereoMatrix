Overview

This single-page document details passive resistive matrices to derive:

  • Phantom center (L+R summing resistors)
  • Ambient rears (Hafler matrix using L−R)
  • Active sub feed (high-impedance divider)

No DSP, no additional amps—just simple open-hardware under GPL-3.0-or-later.

Mode 2.0 — Direct Stereo

Baseline stereo: connect amplifier Left +/− and Right +/− directly to front speakers. Observe polarity and cable quality.

Mode 2.1 — Active Subwoofer Feed

Use a high-impedance divider to feed an active subwoofer’s line-in from speaker outputs.

  • 10 kΩ series from L+ (and R+) to divider node → 1 kΩ to ground → RCA.
  • Mono sum: two 10 kΩ resistors into one node, then 1 kΩ to ground.
  • Sub must accept high-level inputs or use transformer isolation on BTL/Class-D amps.

Mode 3.0 — Phantom Center

Sum L+ and R+ via equal power resistors to create a center channel. Requires common-ground amplifier.

  • 8.2 Ω from L+ to Center+ (15–25 W)
  • 8.2 Ω from R+ to Center+ (15–25 W)
  • Optional shunt 8.2–10 Ω across Center driver (25 W).
  • Center– to common ground only if amplifier shares ground.

Mode 4.0 — Ambient Rears (Hafler)

Wire rear speakers between L+ and R+ to extract the L−R difference signal. Adds natural ambience from stereo material.

  • Rear driver between L+ and R+ (no connection to negatives).
  • Optional series resistor 3.3–10 Ω (10–25 W) or 8 Ω L-pad (50 W).
  • Compatible with BTL/Class-D amps (negatives remain isolated).

Mode 5.0 — Combined Center & Rears

Enable both phantom center and Hafler rears for a fuller spatial field. Use separate switches for each matrix.

Mode 5.1 — Full Surround + Sub

Combines 5.0 matrix with the 2.1 sub feed. Set sub crossover (60–100 Hz) and phase (0/180°) to integrate with fronts. Not discrete surround, but highly immersive.

Bill of Materials

  • 8.2 Ω power resistors (15–25 W) × 2
  • 3.3–10 Ω resistors (10–25 W) × 2 for rears
  • Optional 8.2–10 Ω shunt (25 W) for center
  • 10 kΩ & 1 kΩ (¼ W) resistors for sub divider
  • Terminal posts: IN L/R, FRONT L/R, CENTER, REAR L/R
  • RCA jacks for sub outputs
  • DPDT/4PDT switches or rotary selector
  • Ventilated enclosure, AWG speaker wire, heat-resistant hardware
