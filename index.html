Quick Tips for Neophytes

  • Always match speaker impedance to your amplifier’s spec.
  • Identify “+” and “–” poles by color or markings.
  • Avoid paralleling more than two speakers on one channel.
  • Use ferrite beads on cables to reduce EMI.

FAQ

Q: What is impedance?
A: Electrical resistance (Ω) seen by your amplifier.
Q: Can I mix different speaker impedances?
A: It’s not recommended—stick to identical ohm ratings.
Q: Do I need a special amp for bridged mode?
A: Yes, your amplifier must support bridge output.

Glossary

Impedance (Ω)
Opposition to AC current, combining resistance, inductance, and capacitance.
Ohm
Unit of electrical resistance (Ω). A 1 Ω resistor allows 1 A at 1 V.
Crossover
Filter network (L/C) that splits audio frequencies between drivers.
Bridge
Wiring mode where amplifier outputs are paralleled for higher voltage swing.
